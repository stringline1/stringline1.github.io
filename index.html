Hyunseon Won

MS/PhD Student in Applied Artificial Intelligence at Sungkyunkwan University

I am a researcher focused on Human-AI Interaction, exploring how AI systems can be designed to meet real-world needs across diverse domains. My approach begins with understanding people through interviews with various stakeholders, identifying opportunities where AI can make a meaningful impact, and designing systems that bridge human needs with technological capabilities. My current work centers on developing multi-modal learning and computer vision systems to analyze and advance Human-AI interaction research.
